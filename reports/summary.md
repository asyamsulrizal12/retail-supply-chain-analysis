# Summary Report

## Key Findings
- **Forecasting**: Prophet excels in long-term trend detection; XGBoost provides strong short-term accuracy; LSTM captures long-term dependencies; GRU offers stability
- **Segmentation**: Clear customer/product clusters enable targeted strategies
- **Returns Analysis**: Identified high-return categories with actionable reduction strategies
- **Optimization**: Reduced logistics costs by ~ 120K compared to baseline, while maintaining 100% service level
- **Simulation**: Monte Carlo revealed variability in costs (2.3M–2.7M), service levels (~ 94–100%), and profits (240K–280K)

## Strategic Insights
- Optimization ensures efficiency without compromising service
- Simulation highlights risk exposure and the need for contingency planning
- Forecasting and segmentation provide foresight for growth and customer targeting

## Recommendations
- **Adopt optimization-driven allocation strategies** to reduce costs
- **Integrate simulation into planning** to prepare for uncertainty
- **Use forecasting models with scenario planning** for balanced decision-making 
- **Continuously refine models** with updated data to ensure resilience and adaptability
